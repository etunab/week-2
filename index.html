My College Journey

College has been an amazing journey for me, filled with learning and personal growth. I have discovered a passion for graphic design and creativity, which has guided my academic path. Studying communication design has opened doors to so many exciting opportunities, including the internship opportunity I'm experiencing right now and has been both challenging and rewarding.

"The only way to do great work is to love what you do." - Steve Jobs

As I approach graduation, I am excited about the prospect of working in this industry. My goal is to become a creative director for one of my favorite brands, Balenciaga , where I can apply my skills to create innovative designs that haven't been on the fashion market yet.
My name is Etuna Bekauri, and I look forward to the next chapter of my life.
